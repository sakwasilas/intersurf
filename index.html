About Us

Intersurf Limited is one of Kenya’s leading IT, network, and communication service providers for homes and businesses. Since 2006, we’ve grown from a small cabling company into a nationwide technology partner.

We specialize in delivering fast, secure, and innovative internet solutions designed to meet the modern digital landscape.
